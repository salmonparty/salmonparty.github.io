Welcome to The Salmon Party, if you vote for us it'll go swimmingly.

We have 3 main beliefs:


If I am elected, I would attempt to establish Free Universal healthcare. Although universal health care isn't the best, it establishes a base for healthcare, and makes it cheaper to get higher level healthcare later. It is important to keep healthcare as available as possible, to help people who may not have the money to pay for it to stay healthy, and to keep it affordable for people who can afford it. According to the World Health Organization, about 100 million people are currently being kept in poverty by the costs of healthcare, while over 800 million spent at least 10% of their money to pay for healthcare.



I would also put more affordable post-secondary education in place. I believe it is important to keep post-secondary education as affordable as possible because student loans make it difficult financially for recent graduates of college, and especially when the loans linger. Making post-secondary education would make it easier for graduates to get a start working and paying living costs. According to data compiled by Forbes and CNBC, the debt total(data taken from over 44 million americans) is $1.52 trillion as of 2018, an increase from previous years. Though this is also potentially related to the increase in graduates from college, it still shows an increase in the cost of loans.



My last main focus would be to increase protection of water resources, to make sure the country has clean water for drinking, trade, recreation, and water life. These water resources are important to tourism and recreation, some even being the water that you drink. As well, if our larger bodies of water aren’t protected, the things living in those waters will die. Even when not directly polluted, it’s important to know your activity’s effect on the water to make your life as clean as possible. There’s a multitude of examples of water pollution indirectly affecting lives, such as pollution causing a large number of carp, which are economically important to the country, to die in Iraq, as told by ABC news. It’s important to protect our water so we can contain our pollution problems before the same happens here.
